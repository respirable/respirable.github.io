Hyper-Literalism Tarot

How does Primera actually read the Tarot? Literally. Not spiritually, not metaphorically — literally. Draw a card and find out what it actually says, with no mysticism attached. (Now using Lenormand! Or... Lemonade, as I keep reading it.)

Note from Primera (If you don't know who Primera is, read respirable's anthology): This tool reads Lenormand cards the way any honest person would (and by any, I mean myself only) by taking the imagery and text at face value. No metaphysical assumptions are made. If a card shows a dog, it means dog. If a card shows a ring, it means a ring. Whether that's useful to you is your problem, not mine.
A few things to note:
  1. If a card's reading contradicts that of a previous card, that's not my fault. It's the computer's fault (or maybe yours, I don't know).
  2. Each card has exactly ONE meaning. No more, no less. So... you'll likely only need to use this utility once.
  3. If any of the readings coincide with your real life events, that's probably your fault. I've tried my best to make it sound as impossible as possible.
  4. I know some Tarot/"Lemonade" experts will come here and say Lenormand is fortune-telling and not tarot. To that I say: They are the same in my eyes.
