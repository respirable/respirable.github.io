respirable

anthology

a collection of short stories about my life (in Vietnamese), told through Geometry Dash characters. i wrote these stories intending to challenge the concept of a story, and to also give people an idea of how lonely i am (and how naive i am).

when the stories are finished (greyed-out stories are not finished), i hope you guys will find it one of the most interesting/unique thing you have ever read. i'm still a noob at writing, so... deal with the questionable word choice.

(pro tip: read the stories in order. some stories introduce new things that may be crucial in the next ones. don't be like me and skip a few stories)

(also another tip: if you felt like translating these stories to other languages, then great! come back later when the stories are released)
